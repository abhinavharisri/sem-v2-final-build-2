Technical terms explained
CMH
Cubic metres of air moved per hour.
Static pressure
Resistance the fan overcomes, published in mm-wc.
RPM
Impeller or motor revolutions per minute.
dBA
A-weighted sound level measured in decibels.
Phase
Single-phase (1Ø) or three-phase (3Ø) electrical supply.

* All specifications subject to ±5% tolerance. Contact SEM for custom configurations.
